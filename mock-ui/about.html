Our Story

Grown From a Small Backyard Nursery Into Golden Aura

Since 2010, we've been sourcing, growing, and nurturing ornamental plants for homes, offices, and gardens across the city — one pot at a time.

Founders in the Nursery
Nursery Greenhouse Photo
How It Started

A Passion for Plants, Passed Down

Golden Aura began as a small family-run backyard nursery, growing native flowering plants for neighbours. Over 16 years, it's grown into a trusted ornamental plant destination — but the hands-on, personal approach to plant care hasn't changed.

Every plant that leaves our nursery is hand-selected, health-checked, and potted by our in-house team before it reaches you.

Our Practices

How We Grow & Source

🌱 In-House Propagation

Over 60% of our stock is propagated on-site, ensuring stronger root systems and better acclimatization.

🤝 Trusted Growers Network

Rare and exotic varieties are sourced from a small network of verified regional growers.

🚫 No Chemical Shortcuts

We avoid aggressive growth chemicals — healthier plants that settle into your home faster.
