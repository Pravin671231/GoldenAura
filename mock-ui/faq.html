FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find your answer here? Send us a message or WhatsApp us directly.

Do I need to water my new plant immediately after bringing it home?
Let it settle for a day before watering, unless the soil feels completely dry. Most plants are watered before they leave our nursery.
Do you deliver plants and pots?
Yes — same-city delivery is available for a small fee (see Services). Larger plants and landscaping materials include installation on request.
What if my plant doesn't survive?
We offer a replacement/consultation within 7 days of purchase for plants that show signs of pre-existing disease — bring it in or WhatsApp us a photo first.
Do you serve areas outside Coimbatore city?
Landscaping and AMC services currently cover Coimbatore and nearby suburbs. Contact us with your location and we'll confirm availability.
Can I get a custom garden design without buying plants immediately?
Yes — design consultations are billed separately from plant purchases. See the Landscaping & Garden Design service for details.
How do I know how much light my room gets?
Check our Care Guide for a simple light-level test, or send us a photo of your space on WhatsApp and we'll recommend plants.
